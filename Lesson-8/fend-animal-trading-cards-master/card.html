Asian Elephant Source

The four grinding teeth (molars) wear out and are replaced by new teeth from the back of the jaws; the Asian elephant gets through six sets of teeth during its lifetime.

  • What they eat: Vegetation, fruit, twigs, small branches, bark and roots.
  • Habitat: Mountains and forest.
  • Where they live: India, Sri Lanka, Malaysia, Indonesia and Southern China.
  • Threats: Habitat loss, degradation, fragmentation and poaching.
